About Me

I'm passionate about programming and mathematics. I embarked on my academic journey by studying mathematics at the university for four years. During my first year, I was introduced to programming with C#, an experience that I truly enjoyed. Unfortunately, due to the demands of my mathematics studies, I couldn't dedicate as much time to programming as I would have liked. Nevertheless, I persevered, and upon graduation from the university with honors, I decided to shift my focus toward my true passion: programming.

My journey into the world of programming has been diverse. I began by mastering website layout using HTML, CSS, and delving into JavaScript. My curiosity led me to explore Python and data analysis libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib. I was particularly intrigued by machine learning and took introductory courses to expand my knowledge in this field. Additionally, I dabbled in web development by creating websites using Django.

Over the past year, my intensive focus has been on programming with the C# language. I've actively engaged with various learning resources, including Udemy courses, Linkedin courses and YouTube tutorials. During this time, I successfully completed several projects using WPF and Windows Forms. Furthermore, I've gained proficiency in working with databases, including PostgreSQL and SQL.

I also attempted Android application development using the Kotlin language, which I found particularly enjoyable. Kotlin's similarities to both Python and C#, which I studied for a long time, made it an exciting experience for me.

My dedication to programming continues to drive my learning journey, and I'm excited to tackle new challenges and projects in the field.
